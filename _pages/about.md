I am broadly interested in Black Politics and its shifting dynamics in the aftermath of the Civil Rights Movement. My dissertation project focuses on the Congressional Black Caucus and why, in spite of increased influence and power within Congress, they have been unable to achieve more landmark gains for Black communities.

I have additional research on the interplay between anti-Black racism, maternal health, and the unique features of the American healthcare system.
